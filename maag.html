April 18–19, 2026 · Clemson University

Overview

The Meeting on Applied Algebraic Geometry (MAAG) is a regional gathering which attracts participants primarily from the South-East of the United States. MAAG 2025 takes place at Auburn University on April 19-20, 2025. Previous meetings took place at Georgia Tech in 2015, 2018, 2019, and 2023, at Clemson in 2016, and at Auburn in 2025.

Organizers: Michael Burr, John Cobb, Kisun Lee, Anton Leykin, Julia Lindberg, Luke Oeding
