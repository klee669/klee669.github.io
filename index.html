Here is my CV as of March 2024.
I am a post doctoral fellow in School of Mathematical and Statistical Science at Clemson University. My mentor is Michael Burr.
Before I joined Clemson, I was a SEW visiting assistant professor at the University of California, San Diego mentored by Jiawang Nie.
I received my Ph.D degree from Georgia Tech under the supervision of Anton Leykin.
Pronouns : he, him, his

Research Interests

My research interests are in the fields of nonlinear algebra and applied algebraic geometry. They broadly include :
  • Algorithms in numerical algebraic geometry :
    homotopy continuation, numerical certification
  • Applications in numerical algebraic geometry :
    game theory, algebraic statistics, combinatorics
  • Polyhedral geometry, tropical geometry, tensor rank problems
