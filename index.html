Research Interests

My research interests are in the fields of nonlinear algebra and applied algebraic geometry. They broadly include :
  • Algorithms in numerical algebraic geometry :
    homotopy continuation, numerical certification
  • Applications in numerical algebraic geometry :
    game theory, algebraic statistics, combinatorics
  • Polyhedral geometry, tropical geometry, tensor rank problems

Axiom 3. Mathematics is a powerful, malleable tool that can be shaped and used differently by various communities to serve their needs.
Axiom 4. Every student deserves to be treated with dignity and respect.
